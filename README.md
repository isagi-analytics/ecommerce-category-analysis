# E-commerce Category Performance Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes how sales performance changes across product categories over time. The goal is to identify which categories are growing, which are declining, and whether the overall balance of revenue across the platform is shifting.

Understanding these patterns helps businesses detect early signs of instability and focus on the categories that have the greatest impact on performance.

## Business Problem
Although overall revenue remained relatively stable, the business suspected that performance might be shifting across individual product categories. They wanted to understand whether certain categories were underperforming or showing signs of decline.

Identifying these shifts is important because changes at the category level can directly impact overall performance and influence where the business should focus its efforts.

## Approach
The data was structured into a monthly view for each product category to make trends over time clearly visible. For each category, total revenue was calculated along with how it changes from month to month and how much it contributes to overall platform revenue.

This approach makes it possible to compare categories consistently over time, identify patterns of growth or decline, and detect shifts in how revenue is distributed across the platform.

## Key Insights
The electronics category showed a consistent decline over multiple months, indicating a sustained drop in performance rather than a temporary fluctuation. This suggests a potential structural issue that may require further investigation.

In contrast, the personal care category experienced a sharp increase in revenue followed by a return to its previous baseline. This pattern indicates a temporary spike rather than sustained growth, suggesting the change was likely driven by short-term factors.

## Tools Used
The analysis was completed using SQL for data extraction and Pandas for further analysis.
